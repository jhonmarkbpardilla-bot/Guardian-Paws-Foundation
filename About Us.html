About Us

Our Mission

We are dedicated to rescuing, rehabilitating, and find homes for animals in need. Started in 2020, Guardian Paws Foundation has grown from a small group of passionate animal lovers to a full- fledged rescue organization.

Our team of veterinarians, behaviorists, and volunteers work all day to make sure each animal that comes through our place receives the best possible care. We believe that every animal deserves a chance at a happy, healthy life.

"Compassion in action, one paw at a time."
